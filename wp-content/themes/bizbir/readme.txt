== Description ==

Bizbir is is a powerful, creative, perfect  and feature rich multi-purpose Business HTML template. It is suitable for business, portfolio, digital agencies and general corporate website. It is designed with great attention to details, flexibility and performance thus looks stunning on all types of screens and devices. Bizbir comes with creative and well-organized sections.
